Name: Sebastian Henry

Background
  • Date of Birth: 10/23/1990
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Worcester, UK
  • Work place: Edge Hill University, Faculty of Education, Teacher
  • School: University of Cambridge
  • Technology Level: Height
Main Points
    He works with Zoom meetings app, Edmodo web app, and Google Classroom in order to manage his student's classes and use Google Drive in order to keep his resources organized. He works online with teachers from the entire world. He has to manage a lot of virtual sheets and resources and for this, he dislikes the chaos. He wants all things to be in their place.
Goals
  • Practical Goals: To be as efficiently as possible; to be organized
  • Personal Goals: To became more educated
  • Business Goals: To improves also his teaching skills and methods
Frustrations and Pain Points

Some of the difficulties the user has with the product.

  • Being an organized person he dislikes to see a bunch o information or a lot of things on the same page.
Detailed Description

Sebastian Henry is a highly educated person, an alumnus of the University of Cambridge, UK. He is interested in helping other teachers to improve their teaching skills; this is the reason for he currently works at the Faculty of Education of Edge Hill University an online university. He wants all teachers to be great teachers. He likes to be efficient and organized.


Because the University it's an online one, he works every day with technology and he has good skills in using software tools. He appreciates the technology because it provides the possibility of connecting a lot of o people from different locations very easily.

Scenarios
  1. Sebastian Henry will create a class in the EduLink application in order to manage one of his groups of students and will invite students from that group through an email invitation from the app.
  2. Sebastian Henry will create an assignment for his class in the EduLink application and after the deadline will come to see the students responses and will notate each student according to his answer.
  3. Sebastian Henry will send a message with some advcice to one of his students.
  4. Sebastian Henry uploads course resources to the EduLink file management system so you can keep them organized, safe, and easy to share with students.
  5. He will consult the calendar page to find the schedule of the following courses.
